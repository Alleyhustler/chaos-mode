Introducing Stricken

15/11/2020

Stricken is a dead simple looking Zonelet style inspired by Low Tech Magazine and using dithering textures from Strike.

"WOW! I love when quotes from other sources can be clearly parceled out of a larger body of text."

--Somebody, idk (2020).

It looks great with the theme (and loads quicker) if you convert your images to 1-bit too!

In short, this theme is trying to be:

  • Minimalist
  • Easy to use
  • Quick-loading
  • Low file size
  • Cute!

Well, that's all for now! Bye!
