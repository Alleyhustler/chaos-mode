About me

I'm Em! I'm a writer, researcher and curator interested in experimental games, sci-fi and the history of technology, & distribution platforms in the arts. Most of my writing, curatorial work and more professional info can be found here. If you found your way to this site, welcome! This is a more casual and free-form type of space where I'll be experimenting with my interests in Low-Tech web, HTML, CSS and coding, as well as throwing in some of my less fully-baked thoughts on articles I read or games I play, zines I read, and general life stuff!

I have separate mini-feeds for gigs I go to, books I read, and tarot card draws and interpretation. My game and zine releases can be found on itch.io, and I also log films I've been watching on Letterboxd. I haven't had comments on any of my blogs for over a decade. If you want to get in touch, you can email me.

This site used to be a Zonelet but is now running on Strawberry Starter! Thanks to Marina Kittaka and Kate Bagenzo for making such inspiring tools.

Be sure to check out the Links page for other sites I find inspiring as well as a list of blogs I check regularly.
